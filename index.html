Company Strength

China Jiangsu International Chemical Co., Ltd. (CJI Chemical) is a state-owned professional international trading company established in 1984. As a subsidiary of the CJI Group, we have built a reputation for excellence in the fine chemical industry over the past 40 years.

We are a certified AEO Advanced Enterprise, which grants us priority in customs clearance and international logistics. This status reflects our commitment to compliance, safety, and operational excellence.

Global Logistics & Shipping

We maintain strategic long-term partnerships with major global shipping lines including MAERSK, COSCO, MSC, and CMA CGM. Our expertise in hazardous chemical handling and multimodal transport ensures that your products reach any global destination safely and on time.

Supported by our state-of-the-art warehousing facilities, we provide comprehensive supply chain solutions tailored to the specific needs of the pharmaceutical and industrial chemical sectors.
