Our Heritage

China Jiangsu International Chemical Co., Ltd. (CJI Chemical) is a state-owned professional international trading company established in 1984. As a subsidiary of the CJI Group, we have built a reputation for excellence over the past 40 years.

We are a certified AEO Advanced Enterprise, reflecting our commitment to compliance, safety, and operational excellence in global chemical trade.
